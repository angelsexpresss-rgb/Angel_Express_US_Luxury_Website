Our Promise To Chauffeurs

We want drivers to feel respected, informed, and supported.

Respect

Angel Express treats chauffeurs as professional service partners who represent the company’s ride standard.

Professional Support

Our owner operations system helps monitor trips, passenger needs, ride progress, and support requests.

Fast Payouts

Driver payout is designed around Stripe Connect, with Zelle and Cash App used as backup payout contact options.

Growth Opportunities

As Angel Express grows, approved chauffeurs may access more route opportunities, ratings, and driver levels.

Our Expectations

Angel Express chauffeurs must protect the passenger experience.

We are building a clean, safe, and reliable private transportation brand. Every chauffeur must help maintain that standard.

Clean Vehicle

Vehicle should be clean, organized, comfortable, and ready for passengers, luggage, and long-distance travel.

Professional Appearance

Chauffeurs should present themselves respectfully and maintain a polished, passenger-friendly appearance.

Safe Driving

Chauffeurs must drive responsibly, obey traffic laws, avoid distractions, and prioritize passenger safety.

Excellent Service

Communication, punctuality, courtesy, and professionalism are required on every Angel Express ride.
