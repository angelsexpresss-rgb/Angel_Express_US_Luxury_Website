Privacy & Data Protection

Your information matters.

Angel Express is committed to protecting the privacy and security of passengers, chauffeurs, and website visitors. This policy explains how information is collected, used, stored, and protected across the Angel Express ecosystem.

1. Information We Collect

Angel Express may collect information including:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Pickup and drop-off locations
  • Trip dates and times
  • Emergency contact information
  • Ride preferences
  • Driver application information
  • Trip history and ratings
  • Vehicle information (for chauffeurs)

2. How We Use Your Information

Information is used to:

  • Process ride bookings
  • Assign chauffeurs
  • Provide ride tracking
  • Send confirmations and invoices
  • Improve customer support
  • Verify driver eligibility
  • Enhance safety and operational oversight
  • Manage passenger and chauffeur accounts

3. Passenger App Data

The Angel Express Passenger App may store:

  • Profile information
  • Emergency contacts
  • Ride preferences
  • Trip history
  • Ratings and feedback
  • Push notification tokens
  • Booking activity

Passengers control the information they provide and may request updates to their profile information.

4. Chauffeur App Data

The Angel Express Driver App may store:

  • Driver profile information
  • Vehicle details
  • License and approval information
  • Trip activity
  • Driver ratings
  • Earnings records
  • GPS location updates while active on trips

Location information is used solely for trip management, navigation, safety, and operational oversight.

5. Location & Live Tracking

Angel Express uses location information during active rides to:

  • Support navigation
  • Provide trip progress updates
  • Improve passenger safety
  • Enable owner operational monitoring
  • Assist in emergency situations

Location tracking is associated with active ride operations and is not intended for unrelated monitoring purposes.

6. Payments

Angel Express may use third-party payment providers including Stripe and Stripe Connect. Payment information is processed through those providers according to their security and privacy standards. Angel Express does not store full payment card information on its systems.

7. Data Security

Angel Express uses commercially reasonable safeguards to protect customer and chauffeur information. Access to operational systems is limited to authorized personnel.

8. Information Sharing

Angel Express does not sell personal information. Information may be shared only when necessary to:

  • Complete ride services
  • Process payments
  • Respond to legal requirements
  • Address safety concerns
  • Provide customer support

9. Data Retention

Trip records, invoices, ratings, and operational records may be retained for business, tax, safety, legal, and customer support purposes.
