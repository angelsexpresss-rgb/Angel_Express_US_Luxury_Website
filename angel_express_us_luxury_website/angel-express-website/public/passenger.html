How It Works

A simple booking journey from request to arrival.

01 Estimate Fare

Enter your pickup, drop-off, trip type, date, time, passengers, and luggage.

02 Confirm Booking

Review your trip details and submit the booking request to Angel Express operations.

03 Driver Assigned

Angel Express reviews the ride and assigns a chauffeur when the trip is confirmed.

04 Ride Tracking

Use the passenger app to follow ride updates, chauffeur status, and trip progress.

05 Ride Complete

Payment is collected after ride completion, and your trip history stays available in the app.
