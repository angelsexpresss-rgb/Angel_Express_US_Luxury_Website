Passenger confirmation: Passengers are responsible for reviewing the pickup location, destination, date, time, passenger count, luggage information, and contact details before confirming a ride.

Passengers should request cancellations or changes as early as possible. A cancellation, no-show, or material last-minute change may result in a reasonable fee when a chauffeur has been assigned, has begun traveling to the pickup point, has arrived, or when Angel Express has incurred nonrefundable costs.

Any applicable cancellation or waiting-time charge should be communicated before collection whenever reasonably possible. A passenger who believes a charge is incorrect may contact support for review.

Angel Express is not responsible for delays caused by traffic, severe weather, road closures, law-enforcement activity, airport operations, mechanical emergencies, public events, passenger lateness, incorrect booking information, or other circumstances beyond reasonable control. We will make reasonable efforts to communicate material delays and arrange alternatives when practical.

4. Passenger Responsibilities and Conduct

  • Provide accurate identity, contact, pickup, destination, passenger, and luggage information.
  • Be ready and reachable at the scheduled pickup time.
  • Use seat belts and follow lawful safety instructions.
  • Treat chauffeurs, staff, other passengers, and vehicles respectfully.
  • Do not engage in threatening, discriminatory, abusive, fraudulent, illegal, or unsafe conduct.
  • Do not carry unlawful items, hazardous materials, or items that cannot be transported safely.
  • Disclose accessibility needs, child-seat needs, service-animal needs, or other material requirements early enough for planning.
  • Pay authorized charges associated with the booking.

Angel Express may refuse, suspend, or end service when reasonably necessary to protect safety, prevent fraud, comply with law, or address serious misconduct.

Minors

A person under 18 may not independently create an account or request transportation unless permitted by Angel Express and authorized by a parent, legal guardian, school, or other responsible adult. Additional documentation or supervision requirements may apply.

6. Payments, Refunds and Chauffeur Payouts

Angel Express may accept approved electronic or manually coordinated payment methods. Payment timing may vary by booking and may occur before, during, or after a trip as shown in the booking confirmation or payment request.

Payment processors may collect and process payment-card or financial information directly. Angel Express does not intend to store complete payment-card numbers on its own application database.

Refund requests are reviewed based on the booking, service delivered, payment status, documented charges, and relevant circumstances. Approved refunds may require processing time imposed by the payment provider or financial institution.

Chauffeur compensation and company revenue shares are governed by the applicable chauffeur agreement and trip record. The presently intended standard split for eligible trip revenue is 70% to the chauffeur and 30% to Angel Express, but exclusions, fees, refunds, reversals, disputes, taxes, incentives, or a written agreement may affect the final amount.
